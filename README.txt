                               ABOUT NEPAL
NEPAL (Nepali: नेपाल) is a country in South Asia between India and China. Mount Everest, the highest mountain in the world, and the Himalaya Mountains are in Nepal.
Nepal is a country of highly diverse and rich geography, culture, religions and political instability.
